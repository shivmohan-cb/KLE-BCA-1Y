Upma Recipe

Steps To Follow

  1. Roast Suji
  2. Chop all vegetable you need
  3. Put oil in vessel
  4. Put curry leaves, musturd, jeera, peanut,kaju and chopped vegetable
  5. Put one cup water into the vessel
  6. Add roasted Suji in it
  7. Boil for 5 mins until ready to serve
